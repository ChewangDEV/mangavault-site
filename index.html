Anime Fun Facts

One Piece Fun Fact

Eiichiro Oda already decided the ending before chapter 1. Even if the story changed, the final treasure “One Piece” has never changed since 1997.

Bleach Fun fact

Bleach almost got cancelled multiple times. Tite Kubo was rejected by publishers before Shonen Jump finally accepted the series.

Naruto Fun Fact

Naruto was originally supposed to be a fox. In early drafts by Masashi Kishimoto, Naruto himself was literally a fox disguised as a human, not a ninja.

Sakamoto Days Fun Fact

The author was a former assistant for the JoJo manga team. That’s why the action choreography in the series is extremely cinematic and creative.

Dragon Ball Fun Fact

Goku was originally inspired by the Monkey King. Akira Toriyama based him on Sun Wukong from the Chinese legend Journey to the West.

Jujutsu Kaisen Fun Fact

The creator struggles with drawing Gojo. Gege Akutami once said Gojo is hard to draw consistently, which is why he wears a blindfold.

Attack On Titan Fun Fact

Titans were inspired by drunk people. Hajime Isayama said Titans’ strange movements were based on a drunk customer he once saw at work.

Demon Slayer

Tanjiro almost had a different personality. Early drafts made him much darker and more aggressive, but editors asked the author to make him kinder.

Solo Leveling Fun Fact

The artist worked extreme hours before his death. The illustrator Jang Sung‑rak reportedly worked insane schedules weekly, contributing to health problems.

Chainsaw Man Fun Fact

Denji was designed to represent poverty. Tatsuki Fujimoto intentionally made Denji obsessed with basic things like bread and jam to reflect extreme poverty.
